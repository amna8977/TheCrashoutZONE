The Crashout Zone is a tiny place to be angry without pretending everything is fine. Type it, punch it, send it away, and get back to being a functional human. This project was made for Hack Club BEEST.

Enter the Zone

What happens here?

Combust the burning-hot fury which is you. The zone is a safe place to let it all out, then get back to being a functional human. You can:

  • Type your crashout
  • Use voice crashout
  • Send it into the zone

What is the Zone?

The Crashout Zone is a tiny stress-release machine that is part website, part punching bag, and fully committed to letting the chaos leave your body. It was built for Hack Club BEEST as a small, dramatic reset button for rough moments.

When you are ready, head to the crashout page to type, speak, crashout, or punch your way into the zone.
